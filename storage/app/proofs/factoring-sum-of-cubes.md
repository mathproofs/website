## Statement

The sum of two cubes can be factored as follows.

$$ a^3 + b^3 = (a + b)(a^2 - ab + b^2) $$

## Proof

Take $ (a + b)(a^2 - ab + b^2) $ and expand the brackets. Then cancel like terms.

$$ (a + b)(a^2 - ab + b^2) = $$

$$ a^3 - a^2b + ab^2 + a^2b - ab^2 + b^3 = $$

$$ a^3 + b^3 $$
